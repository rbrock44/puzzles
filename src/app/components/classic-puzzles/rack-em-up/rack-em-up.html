Tilt

Tilt the whole board left or right. Every row slides its balls toward that side, gathering any gaps on the opposite side.

Shift columns

The leftmost and rightmost columns can also shift up or down one row. Shifting doesn't move any balls by itself — it reconnects that edge column to a different row. The next tilt then carries a ball across into (or out of) the row it's now aligned with. This is the only way to mix balls between rows.
